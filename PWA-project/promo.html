What is Planet Sights?

Planet Sights is an educational app that lets you explore our solar system from anywhere. Tap any planet, star, or moon to see its image, learn fun facts, and listen to its real NASA-recorded sounds. Built as a Progressive Web App, you can install it on your phone and use it offline!

How to Install

📱 On iPhone

  1. Open the app in Safari
  2. Tap the Share button at the bottom
  3. Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen"
  4. Tap "Add" in the top right

🤖 On Android

  1. Open the app in Chrome
  2. Tap the menu (three dots in top right)
  3. Tap "Install app" or "Add to Home Screen"
  4. Tap "Install"

💻 On Desktop (Chrome)

  1. Open the app in Chrome
  2. Look for the install icon in the address bar
  3. Click it and confirm "Install"
  4. The app opens in its own window!

🌐 Use in Browser
